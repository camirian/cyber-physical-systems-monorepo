**Goal:** Complete the full specialization with optimized, documented solutions.
- Ensure all specialization notebooks are optimized and documented for quick reference.

## Version N: Make it scale (Scaled Monopoly)

- An agent-driven tutoring system that provides custom, interactive ML problems based on the learner's specific engineering background.
- Automatically updates content as new foundational papers and models are released.
